Welcome to Ridge Tutors

A community-driven platform dedicated to connecting families in the Bernards Township School District with motivated Ridge High School students offering tutoring services.

Find a Tutor

Our Mission

Ridge Tutors was created as a free, local resource to foster academic support and mentorship within our community. We aim to:

  • Empower Ridge High School students to share their knowledge and gain valuable experience by offering tutoring services.
  • Provide families with easy access to qualified, peer-based academic assistance for their children.
  • Strengthen community ties by facilitating meaningful educational relationships between students of different ages.

How It Works

For Families

Browse our directory of Ridge High School tutors, each verified as a student in good standing. Tutors offer services in various subjects and set their own hourly rates. You can contact tutors directly to discuss your child's needs and schedule sessions that work for you.

For Tutors

If you're a Ridge High School student interested in tutoring, you can create a profile detailing your subjects of expertise, availability, and rates. Our platform provides you with the opportunity to gain experience, earn income, and make a positive impact in your community.
